Start Project

Finding difficulty in making your app a dream come true? Then this Tutorial will explain you step by step how to develop your app without much hassle.

Just follow the simple steps provided by us and you will have your app made in a jiffy.

Adding a slider to your home page could never be this easy.

  1. Click on the provided images or click on upload file to upload the images of your choice.
  2. If you have selected, provided images then you will come across a edit image Page.

    • Add a title or caption to your image through the help of title option.

    • You can see the preview of slider image. Incase you don't like that image then you can even upload an image of your choice by clicking on the upload file button.

    • Incase you want those sliders to take you on other article/page then you can do so by using the link option.

    • In future if you don't want a particular slide to exist but only for short duration then no need to delete and then again develop it ,when you have the enable/disable option at your disposal.

    • If you wish to save the changes made then click on save or on cancel.

  3. Next you will observe the set home content section. Best part of it is the WYSIWYG ("what you see is what you get") property. That is if you copy content from anywhere still you will be able to paste it in the content section.

Navigation

Navigation forms the base of your site as it will provide access to other pages/articles of your site

  1. You will find the Createbutton at the right side which will help you start off with your navigation.
  2. Now you will observe the create navigation page in which their are various options further available.

    • NAME-Here you need to fill in the name of your individual articles.

      eg:HOME,ABOUT,CAREERS etc.

    • SELECT ICON:Here you are been provided with a list of icons from which you can choose to use as a logo for each navigation name.

    • ENABLE:It might happen that you don't anymore want a particular article to appear on your site then you can simply disable it with the enable(ON)/disable(OFF)button.

    • LINK:You can link that article further to any other article that you wish.If also later you wish like changing the link you can do it with ease by simply clicking on the CHANGE LINK.

      In links you further have the option of linking all the photo galleries/video galleries or a single photo gallery/video gallery and placing it in the navigation itself.

    • If you wish to save the changes made then click on save or on cancel.

  3. Follow the step 2 again every time you want a new article to be created and which needs to be in navigation.
  4. Once you are done with the CREATE part you will find the entire navigation made by you with further options at the extreme right.

    • STAR SIGN-This sign on click turns into yellow which indicates that,the particular article has become default (i.e Whenever your site loads that article will be the first to appear to user on your site page. )

    • PENCIL SIGN(BLUE COLOURED)-By means of it you can edit your article content. eg:If you no longer like the icon that you had initially used for about us article then click on edit icon(BLUE COLOURED)-->goto select icon-->select another icon from the list provided in dropdown-->click SAVE

    • DELETE SIGN(RED COLOURED): No longer wanted articles can be deleted by clicking on this icon.On clicking a popup will appear that will ask whether you surely want to delete it.

Events

EVENTS-

If you have a certain event organised for your clients then events option is made available, through which your clients will be notified and they will thus be able to attend that event.

  • TITLE-Provide title to your event(eg:Opening ceremony of our new branch)

  • VENUE-Provide the address where the event is going to take place.

  • CONTENT-Add content , basically a short description about the events that are going to take place at the described venue.You can even beautify it by the various options made available to you.

  • START & END DATE-If the function is going to be for long duration ( eg: college fest ) then you can also provide start date and end date.

  • START & END TIME-If the function is going to be for a specific time period then provide the same.

  • EVENT BANNER-The first look of your event( eg:image) can be provided through the UPLOAD FILE button.

There are two ways in which you can provide info of your event in just a click-

  1. Images-Adding multiple event images can be done by means of the UPLOAD FILES button.
  2. Videos-If you have a video to show then it can be done with the help of UPLOAD VIDEO button.
