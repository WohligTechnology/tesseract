Can I integrate self hosted wordpress OR wordpress.com blogs?

Yes. Blazen provides the ability to integrate both SELF HOSTED WORDPRESS and wordpress.com blogs.

One can do it by entering the backend (i.e the dashboard present at left) and enter your wordpress username and Self Hosted Wordpress Blog URL and it will thus be integrated.

Can I get the audio gallery from my soundcloud account?

Absolutely. All you have to do is provide the username of your Soundcloud account. One can do it by:

Click on audio gallery --> SETUP AUDIO GALLERY --> Enter the Soundcloud username And thus you will have access to your audio gallery.
